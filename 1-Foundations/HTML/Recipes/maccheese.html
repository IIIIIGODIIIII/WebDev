Mac & Cheese

Homemade mac and cheese recipe uses a classic mixture of sharp Cheddar and Parmesan. Cheddar is a fantastic base, while Parmesan adds a more complex and salty flavor. Of course, you can adapt this recipe to suit your taste: Gruyere, Monterey Jack, and Gouda are all great options for macaroni and cheese.

  • 1/2 pound elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 6 ounces evaporated milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Fresh black pepper
  • 3/4 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 10 ounces sharp cheddar, shredded
  • 1 ounce Parmesan, grated
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large pot of boiling, salted water cook the pasta to al dente.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, in a separate pot, melt the butter.
  4. Whisk in the eggs, milk, hot sauce, salt, pepper, and mustard.
  5. Stir in 3/4 of the cheese.
  6. After the pasta is cooked, drain and fold it into the cheese mixture.
  7. Pour the mixture into a casserole dish and top with remaining cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake for another 30 minutes or until top is golden brown.
