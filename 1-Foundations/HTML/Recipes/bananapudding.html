Banana Pudding

This is a delicious banana pudding recipe! It is a great dessert for any occasion and is sure to be a hit with your family and friends. Enjoy!

  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 1/2 cups cold water
  • 1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 (12 ounce) package vanilla wafers
  • 4 bananas, sliced
  1. In a medium bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk and water. Mix well.
  2. Add pudding mix and blend well.
  3. Chill for 5 minutes.
  4. In a large bowl, beat heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  5. Fold pudding mixture into whipped cream.
  6. Spoon 1 cup of pudding mixture into a 2 1/2 quart glass serving bowl.
  7. Top with one-third each of the vanilla wafers, bananas, and remaining pudding.
  8. Repeat layers twice, ending with pudding.
  9. Chill before serving.
